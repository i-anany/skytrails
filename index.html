Travel Tips

Packing Essentials

  • Carry a small first-aid kit.
  • Pack light and keep space for souvenirs.
  • Keep important documents in a separate folder.

Money & Safety

  • Always keep some local currency with you.
  • Share your itinerary with family or friends.
  • Use hotel lockers for passports and valuables.

Smart Travel

  • Check the weather forecast before packing.
  • Download offline maps of your destination.
  • Respect local culture, dress codes and rules.
